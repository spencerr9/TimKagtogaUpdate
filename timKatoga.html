Timothy Katoga is a Forbes 30 Under 30 Europe shortlist and an award-winning Marketing and Communications executive, with expertise in spearheading digital transformations, brand identity and development, content strategy, social media, omni-channel growth, and integrated communications.

He was recently included on The Drum’s Future 50 Marketers 2019 list.

Incorporating the full spectrum of marketing channels, his deep understanding of cultural challenges and opportunities in the 24/7 global digital marketplace has steered his career in helping define multi-million-pound organisations. Passionately curious and commercially savvy, he builds consensus among C-suite executives and senior stakeholders to promote transparency and influence positive changes throughout, ensuring businesses stay ahead of the curve in a sustainable way — on a corporate level, in both B2B and B2C sectors.

Timothy has earned a formidable reputation as a trailblazer. At the helm of Digital Marketing initiatives at Headlam Group PLC, he had full responsibilities for 11 companies wholly-owned by a £708million turnover corporation. He led and repositioned the companies’ brand strategy, digital footprint and voice, and brought their marketing initiatives into the 21st century whilst contributing to new revenue streams from his innovative implementations. He also had further interim responsibilities for the Group’s European businesses based in the Netherlands, France, and Switzerland.

Prior to this, he held various positions in the music/entertainment industry at SBTV and MOBO Awards. Other highlights include: working with some of the UK’s top-tier musicians, celebrities and influencers such as Ed Sheeran, KSI, One Direction band, etc. Timothy was also the first person in the UK music/entertainment industry to start a digital street team for various A-List celebrities — a concept which has now been globally adopted by the masses today.

As an advisor to company leaders and executives, he has also provided his skillset to individuals such as Jamal Edwards MBE (Founder/CEO of SBTV), whom Timothy co-wrote a book with entitled “Self Belief: How to Be a Success on Your Own Terms”, which went on to become a number one eBook bestseller in 2013.

Currently, Timothy Katoga is the executive leading all the Digital Marketing and Communications for Europe, Middle East & Africa (EMEA) at Veolia Water Technologies, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Veolia with a turnover €25billion.
