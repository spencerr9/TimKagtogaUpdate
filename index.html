Timothy Katoga

Marketing | Digital | Communications

Content | Brand Strategy | Advisor

London, UK

Tim has earned a formidable reputation as a trailblazer. At the helm, as Head of Digital Marketing & Social Media at Headlam Group plc, he had full responsibilities for 11 companies wholly-owned by a £700million turnover corporation. He spearheaded and repositioned the companies’ brand strategy, digital footprint and voice, and brought their marketing initiatives into the 21st century whilst contributing to new revenue streams from his implementations.

He also had further interim responsibilities for the Group’s European businesses in the Netherlands, France and Switzerland.

Prior to this, he held various positions in the music entertainment sector at SBTV and MOBO Awards, leading their strategy, development, marketing and digital transformation initiatives. Other highlights include; working with some of the UK’s top-tier musicians, celebrities and influencers, such as Ed Sheeran, KSI, One Direction band, etc. Tim was also the first person in the UK music and entertainment industry to start a digital street team for various celebrities; a concept which has now been adopted by the masses today.

As an advisor to company leaders, he has also provided his skillset to individuals such as Jamal Edwards MBE (Founder/CEO of SBTV), whom Tim co-wrote a book with entitled ‘Self Belief: How to Be a Success on Your Own Terms’, which went on to become a number one eBook bestseller.

Currently, Tim leads the Digital and Communications initiatives for UK & Europe at Veolia Water Technologies, a wholly-owned subsidiary by Veolia; a €25billion turnover company with over 300,000 employees worldwide.

Tim was recently shortlisted for Forbes 30 Under 30 Europe.
